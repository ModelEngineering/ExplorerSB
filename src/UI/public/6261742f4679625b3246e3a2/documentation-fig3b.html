This is the general mathematical model for the AE1 model with modifications to enable simulations which are capable of reproducing Figure 3B in the original paper. See the various views available in the right hand column. The simulation experiment for this model can be obtained by loading the corresponding SED-ML document into OpenCOR and executing the simulation. The results of which are shown below. A rendering of the mathematical model itself is available here.

In Figure 3B, the HCO3-/Cl- flux is computed for a range of internal Cl- concentration. Internal and external HCO3- concentrations are fixed at 26 mM; external Cl- concentration is 114 mM; and internal Cl- concentration is varied from 0 to 120 mM.
